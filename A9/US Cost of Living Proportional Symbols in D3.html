United States Cost of Living, 2025

Proportional Symbols Map: housing cost index by state centroid

The symbol size encodes the housing cost index from the expensive-states CSV, and the map positions come from a separate state-centroid table that was joined during data preparation. The top 10 most expensive states by overall composite cost index are highlighted in a different color and include tooltips.

Source data: expensive-states.csv, state centroid coordinates, and us-atlas state boundaries.
