Sao Paulo Municipal Homicides, 2017-2022

Choropleth Map: municipalities in the Sao Paulo metro region of Brazil

This A+ extension maps cumulative homicide incidents recorded between 2017 and 2022 across municipalities in the Sao Paulo metropolitan region. The map operates below the state level and includes an ordered legend plus tooltips for each municipality.

Source data: crimemappingdata municipality boundaries and Sao Paulo homicide records.
