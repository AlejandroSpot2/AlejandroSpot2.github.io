A9 - Geospatial Visualizations in D3

This assignment folder contains four geospatial visualizations that cover the county choropleth, state proportional symbols map, world choropleth, and the A+ extension at a lower-than-state level in another country.

Maps

  • United States county poverty choropleth
  • United States cost of living proportional symbols map
  • World life expectancy choropleth
  • Sao Paulo municipal homicides choropleth

Data Sources

  • USDA ERS county-level poverty estimates
  • MERIC cost of living data series combined with Google state centroid coordinates
  • World Bank life expectancy indicator
  • crimemappingdata Sao Paulo municipality boundaries and homicide records
